General Dentistry

Foundation of Health

Comprehensive oral health care — check-ups, digital X-rays, professional cleaning, fluoride treatments and composite fillings. We recommend a check-up every 6 months to catch problems before they become costly.

  • Comprehensive examination & digital X-rays
  • Professional scaling & polishing
  • Tooth-coloured composite fillings
  • Extractions & emergency care

Orthodontics

For All Ages

Straighten your teeth discreetly with Invisalign clear aligners or traditional braces. Dr. Rohit Shah is a certified Invisalign provider with hundreds of successful cases in Mumbai.

  • Invisalign & clear aligner therapy
  • Metal & ceramic braces
  • Retainers & post-treatment care
  • Virtual progress monitoring

Dental Implants

Permanent Solution

Replace missing teeth with titanium implants that look, feel and function exactly like your natural teeth. We use Nobel Biocare implants and offer same-day temporary crowns.

  • Single tooth & full-arch implants
  • All-on-4 & All-on-6 solutions
  • CBCT-guided surgical planning
  • Bone grafting when needed

Root Canal Treatment

Pain-Free

Modern rotary endodontics makes root canal treatment comfortable and efficient. We save teeth that other clinics might extract — usually in a single appointment.

  • Single-visit RCT in most cases
  • Rotary NiTi file systems
  • Digital apex locators
  • Post & core + crown restoration
