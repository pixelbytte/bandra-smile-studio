A Clinic Built Around You

Bandra Smile Studio was founded in 2015 by Dr. Anjali Mehta after she returned from post-doctoral training in cosmetic dentistry in London. She envisioned a practice that combined international clinical standards with the warmth and trust that Mumbai's communities naturally provide.

What started as a two-chair clinic on Pali Hill has grown into a full-service dental studio — one that attracts patients from across Mumbai, not just because of the results we deliver, but because of how we make people feel through the entire experience.

Today, our team of three MDS-qualified specialists sees over 400 patients a month, handles complex smile transformations, and remains committed to continuing education — attending international conferences yearly to bring the latest techniques back to Bandra.
